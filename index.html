About Me

Hello! I’m Vanessa, and I’ve been running for over five years. What started as a casual interest has grown into one of the most defining parts of who I am. I run not just for fitness, but for clarity, for discipline, for peace—and for the thrill of chasing down a goal.

Distance running pushes me beyond my limits, mentally and physically. I love early morning runs, the feel of pavement underfoot, and the hum of determination in my chest. Whether I’m training for a race or just unwinding from a long day, running is where I feel most alive.

Marathon Milestones

I’ve completed four marathons to date, each one a journey of its own:

  • 🏁 Seattle Marathon – Twice, my hometown course that never gets old.
  • 🇩🇪 Germany – My first international marathon. Fast, flat, and unforgettable.
  • 🌲 Olympia, Washington – Scenic, peaceful, and filled with Northwest charm.
Fun fact: I didn’t grow up running. I found it in adulthood—and it quickly became something I can’t imagine life without.

Trail Running

When I’m not chasing marathon goals, you’ll find me on the trails. I’m passionate about trail running—moving through forests, climbing hills, getting muddy, and breathing fresh mountain air. Trails are where I reconnect with nature and myself.

Trail running has taught me to be patient, to respect terrain, and to find joy in slow progress. Every root and rock is part of the journey.
